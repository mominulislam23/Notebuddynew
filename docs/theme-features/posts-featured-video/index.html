Post's Featured Video

By Rafael Martín Posted in on October 5, 2017 0 Comments 1 min read

Just as in each WordPress post you can indicate a prominent image to illustrate the post, Narratium expands the Video Headers feature of WordPress to allow that you can also add a featured video in each post of the site.

In this way, Narratium adds a new metabox titled “Featured Video” in the post editing page from which you can upload the video you want to appear as a highlight or indicate your url if that video is on YouTube.

The video will appear in the templates in which this feature is available replacing the highlighted image. In case the video can not be loaded due to the inconvenience, the highlighted image will prevail over the video.
